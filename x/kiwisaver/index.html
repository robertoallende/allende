Core Investment Thesis

Behavioral Guidelines

⚠️ What NOT to Do

  • Check portfolio daily or weekly
  • Chase performance or rotate into hot sectors
  • Increase equity beyond 85%
  • Sell during crashes
  • Listen to media or friends’ returns

✓ Commitment to Discipline

  • Annual rebalancing only - Each September
  • Maintain 15% bonds - Even in bull markets
  • Trust the process - 15 years is enough time
  • Rebalancing creates alpha - ~0.5-1% annually

For My Future Panicked Self

When reading this during a crash:

Take a deep breath. This is what we planned for. Bonds are holding up while stocks are on sale.

This crash is your opportunity to buy low.

You have 15 years. This crash is noise. Trust your past calm self who built this portfolio.

Stick to the plan. Future you will thank you.

Annual Review Checklist

  1. Calculate current allocations
  2. Identify positions outside ±2% bands
  3. Execute rebalancing (sell high, buy low)
  4. Verify 50% minimum in US500
  5. Confirm no position exceeds 5%
  6. Review thesis validity
  7. Document lessons learned
