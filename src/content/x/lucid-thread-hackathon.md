## What is Lucid Thread

AI assistants today collaborate on their terms.

Lucid Thread lets users orchestrate multiple AI personas — twenty specialized assistants, or custom ones — with visible memory and real-time document tools, all operating within boundaries defined by the user.

Instead of a single generic assistant, you can decide which perspectives participate in the conversation and what the AI remembers.

### Why hackathons?

In the era of AI, the best way to learn is hands-on.

Hackathons create a very particular environment: time-bound, public, imperfect, and constrained. Over the past year I’ve joined even hackathons and counting.

My goal is to help build a community of people who treat hackathons as a practice for learning and experimentation.

You can read more on [Hackathons as Practice](https://allende.nz/notes/hackathoncop/).
